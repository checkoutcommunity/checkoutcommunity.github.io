About

CheckoutCommunity is a minecraft server with the goal to never reset the world in a free for all no rules pvp environment, with little modification to the vanilla survival gamemode.

The world is 10+ years old, with a size of 24,704 MBs and over 3,145-пиздешь players visiting at least once. The IP to connect is checkoutcommunity.ml
